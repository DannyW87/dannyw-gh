On The Day

The Ceremony will commence at St Nicholas' Church in Whiston at 1pm, followed by the Reception at the Richmond Hotel in Liverpool.

There will be arranged transport on the day going from the ceremony to the reception. You can state whether you will be using this in your RSVP.

The reception has valet parking for any guests using their own transport at £15 (subject to availability), or can use the Q-Park Dale Street located just up the road from the reception at a discounted rate of £8 for 24 hours. Please ask for the discount at reception upon leaving the hotel.

On arrival at the Richmond, there will be drinks and canapés (on request in RSVP), followed by a 3 course meal and a evening buffet in the night.

For any guests wishing to stay the night in the hotel, there is a 20% discount on rooms. Just mention that you are attending the wedding when booking the room.

Our amazing team of professional photographers will capture every aspect of our ceremony. We invite you to sit back, relax and enjoy the moment with us. Please put down all electronic devices and be part of our ceremony with us!

Food Menu

Below is the full menu for the wedding breakfast. In your RSVP please choose one option from each course. Vegetarian meals are listed with (v).

Appetizer

  • Homemade Lancashire cheese & onion tart with peppery rocket & pesto oil (v)
  • Winter vegetable soup with pearl barley (v)
  • Caesar salad with little gem lettuce, crispy bacon, croutons, parmesan & dressing
  • Cheesy garlic bread (children's)

Entrée

  • Breast of chicken stuffed with bacon & savoy cabbage, fondant potato finished with a shallot jus
  • Roast loin of pork with spiced sea salt potatoes, pea and ham hash, piggy stuffing & honey and mustard
  • Butter Bean & Smoked Paprika Loaded Roast Peppers with melting cheese on Honey & Coriander scented Couscous (v)
  • Chicken nuggets, chips & beans (children's)

Dessert

  • Strawberry shortcake tower, heart shaped shortbread, whipped cream & fresh strawberries
  • Tower of profiteroles, filled with Chantilly cream & topped with dark chocolate sauce
  • Chocolate brownie & ice cream (children's)

Canapés will be served on arrival (if requested) and an evening buffet will also be available during the night that will include hot and cold snacks and cake.
